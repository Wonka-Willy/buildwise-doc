## Eligibility & Access

### Member Organizations Only
<Warning>
**Access Requirement:** Tooli is exclusively available to **paying members** of our partner organizations:
- **Embuild** 
- **Bouwunie** 
- **Constructiv**
- **Buildwise** 

**Your professional email must be whitelisted** through your organization's membership database to access Tooli.
</Warning>

### Common Access Issues

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Email Not Recognized" icon="message">
**Message:** "Email address not recognized"

**Solution:**
1. Verify you have an active, **paid membership** with Buildwise, Embuild, Bouwunie, or Constructiv
2. Confirm your email is registered in their member database
3. Contact your organization directly to update your email or verify membership status
4. Non-members can request access, but approval requires manual review
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Login Attempts Blocked" icon="lock">
**Message:** "Too many login attempts"

**Solution:**
1. Wait 15 minutes before trying again
2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser or incognito/private mode
4. Contact support if the issue persists
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Verification Email Issues" icon="clock">
**Problem:** Verification link expired or not received

**Solution:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Request a new verification email (links expire quickly for security)
3. Ensure you're using the correct email address
4. Try registering with your organization's official domain email
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What to Include in Support Requests

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Access Issues">
**Include:**
- Your full name and email address
- Partner organization you're a member of
- Membership ID or account number (if available)
- Screenshots of error messages
- When the issue started occurring
</Tab>

<Tab title="Technical Problems">
**Include:**
- Description of what you were trying to do
- What happened vs. what you expected
- Browser and device information
- Screenshots or error messages
- Steps to reproduce the issue
</Tab>

<Tab title="Feature Questions">
**Include:**
- Your role (contractor, project manager, etc.)
- Type of construction projects you work on
- Specific use case or scenario
- What you're trying to accomplish
</Tab>
</Tabs>
