## Meet Your Construction Team

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="General Agent" icon="message">
*Your first point of contact and navigation guide in Tooli*

### What This Agent Does
<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Primary Role" icon="compass">
- **Helps with all general construction questions**
- **Knows Belgian building codes, regulations and industry standards**
- **Guides you to the right specialist agent** when needed  
- **Handles everyday questions too** (not only construction)
</Card>

<Card title="Knowledge Areas" icon="brain">
- Belgian construction regulations & EPB rules  
- Construction materials, techniques, standards  
- Site organization, safety, good practices  
- General technical and non-technical topics
</Card>
</CardGroup>

### How to Work With This Agent

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Starting Questions">
**Ask anything:**
- *"What is the difference between glass wool and rock wool?"*
- *"Explain U-value requirements in Wallonia."*
- *"What causes basement condensation?"*
- *"Which agent should I use to analyze my quote?"*
</Tab>

<Tab title="Getting Guidance">
**When you're not sure where to start:**
- *"I received a tender—what should I check first?"*
- *"Help me understand these meeting notes."*
- *"What steps should I take before pricing this project?"*

The General Agent will guide you to the correct specialist.
</Tab>
</Tabs>

### What This Agent Cannot Do
- Detailed quote analysis (use **Offer Coach**)
- BoQ/tender checking (use **Tender Checker**)  
- Professional meeting minute formatting (use **Meeting Minutes Writer**)  
- Legal advice (consult qualified professionals)

### Pro Tips
- Use **follow-up questions** to refine answers  
- Ask for **sources or regulation references**  
- Switch to specialists when recommended  
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Offer Coach" icon="check-circle">
*Your quote-quality specialist*

### What This Agent Does
<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Quote Analysis" icon="magnifying-glass">
- **Analyzes your own drafted quotes**  
- **Identifies weaknesses, ambiguities, and inconsistencies**  
- **Checks completeness & coverage**  
- **Highlights operational and contractual risks**
</Card>

<Card title="Quality Areas" icon="shield-check">
- Financial: margin structure, cost coverage  
- Technical: clarity, completeness, conformity  
- Operational: feasibility, planning assumptions  
- Contractual: validity, price revision, terms
</Card>
</CardGroup>

### How to Work With This Agent

<Steps>
<Step title="Upload Your Quote">
**Share your drafted offer:**  
- PDF, Word, Excel  
- Optional: specifications, annexes, general conditions  
</Step>

<Step title="Get Structured Analysis">
You receive:  
- **Summary** of quote scope  
- **Core analysis** (financial, technical, operational, contractual)  
- **Top issues** ranked by importance  
- **Opportunities** to strengthen your offer  
</Step>

<Step title="Iterate and Improve">
Ask follow-ups:  
- *"Which items are unclear?"*  
- *"Where should I add assumptions?"*  
- *"What are the missing documents?"*  
</Step>

<Step title="Refine Your Quote">
Make improvements, re-upload, and let Offer Coach re-check.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Example Input/Output

<Tabs>
<Tab title="What You Upload">
*A drafted quote for a renovation project.*
</Tab>

<Tab title="What You Get">
## Summary  
The quote covers all main works but lacks clarity on transport, waste, and execution deadlines.

## Core Analysis Points  
- **Financial:** Cost structure unclear, missing indirect costs section  
- **Technical:** Several vague item descriptions  
- **Operational:** Execution period not specified  
- **Contractual:** No validity period, no price revision clause  

## Top Issues (Ranked)  
1. Missing validity period  
2. Undefined execution timeline  
3. No explicit waste management provisions  

## Opportunities  
1. Add material choice annex  
2. Clarify what is included/excluded  
3. Add assumptions for coordination  
</Tab>
</Tabs>

### What This Agent Cannot Do
- Create quotes from zero  
- Calculate prices or give financial advice  
- Draft legal clauses  
- Guarantee project success  

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Assuming the agent calculates quantities  
- Mixing labour & material in one line  
- Forgetting to attach annexes  

### Pro Tips
<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Iterative Improvement" icon="repeat">
- Upload multiple quote versions  
- Ask specific questions  
- Compare approaches  
</Card>

<Card title="Follow-up Questions" icon="question">
- *"Where are risks insufficiently described?"*  
- *"What assumptions should I make explicit?"*  
</Card>
</CardGroup>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Tender Checker" icon="search">
*Your BoQ and tender document inspector*

### What This Agent Does
<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Error Detection" icon="bug">
- **Finds calculation mistakes**  
- **Detects duplicates & missing items**  
- **Checks units and quantities**  
- **Flags risky or unclear descriptions**
</Card>

<Card title="Quality Control" icon="clipboard-check">
- Structural completeness  
- Logical consistency  
- Provisional quantities  
- Items requiring client clarification
</Card>
</CardGroup>

### How to Work With This Agent
<Steps>
<Step title="Upload Tender Documents">
BoQ, price sheet, specifications (PDF or Excel).
</Step>

<Step title="Get Systematic Check">
You receive:  
- Summary of issues  
- Calculation anomalies  
- Missing/duplicate items  
- Questions to ask the client  
</Step>

<Step title="Ask Targeted Questions">
- *"Explain the issue in lot 3?"*  
- *"Which items appear duplicated?"*  
- *"What should I clarify before pricing?"*  
</Step>

<Step title="Prepare Clarifications">
Use the findings to create precise questions for the client.
</Step>
</Steps>

### Example Analysis Output

<Tabs>
<Tab title="Input Document">
BoQ for apartment renovation (230 line items).
</Tab>

<Tab title="Results">
## Summary  
- Several unit inconsistencies  
- 3 duplicates detected  
- 14 items without quantification  
- Many vague descriptions requiring clarification  

## Calculation Checks  
- Item 2.13: Total does not match unit × quantity  
- Item 4.02: Unit "m³" used for painting (should be "m²")

## Notable Items  
- "Various adaptations" → unclear scope  
- Items 3.02 & 3.03 appear identical but priced separately  

## Questions for Client  
1. Clarify scope for "various adaptations"  
2. Confirm if 3.02/3.03 are distinct  
3. Provide details for provisional sum items  
</Tab>
</Tabs>

### What This Agent Cannot Do
- Interpret architectural drawings  
- Calculate final prices  
- Provide legal advice  
- Replace a professional quantity surveyor  

### Working Effectively
- Ensure documents are readable  
- Ask specific questions  
- Verify all flagged points  
- Combine with Offer Coach for pricing strategy  
</Accordion>

## Working with Multiple Agents

### Seamless Agent Switching

<Steps>
<Step title="Start with Any Agent">
Choose the agent that fits your first task.
</Step>

<Step title="Switch When Needed">
Click another agent—your context automatically transfers.
</Step>

<Step title="Maintain Context">

</Step>

<Step title="Combine Expertise">
Use multiple agents for complete project workflows.
</Step>

### Example Workflows

<Tabs>
