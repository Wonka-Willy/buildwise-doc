## What Tooli Does

Think of Tooli as a smart assistant that knows construction inside and out. You can ask it questions just like you would ask a colleague, and it gives you helpful answers right away.

<Steps>
<Step title="Ask questions">
Ask in Dutch, French, German, or English--Tooli understands construction terms and Belgian regulations.
</Step>

<Step title="Upload Files">
Add project files, specifications, or plans when you need help reviewing them.
</Step>

<Step title="Get Clear Answers">
Receive explanations in the language that makes sense for your work.
</Step>

<Step title="Ask Follow-Up Questions">
Get more details or clarification whenever you need it.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Your Six Expert Agents
**What's an agent?** Think of each agent as a specialized colleague who's an expert in one specific area of construction work. Instead of one general assistant, you get six specialists - each trained to help with different tasks you face every day.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="General Assistant" icon="Message">
    Your versatile construction companion for any question - from materials and calculations to general advice and industry guidance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Small Works Quote Builder" icon="calculator">
    Transform a client request into a complete quote for small works with assumptions, exclusions and planning.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="BoQ Sanity & Gap Finder" icon="search">
    Check your bills of quantities and estimates to detect errors, duplicates and risk items.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Variation/Extra Work Builder" icon="edit">
    Write formal texts for modifications and additional works with time/cost impact.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Quote Quality Check" icon="check-circle">
    Analyze your own quote and identify weak points, missed aspects and improvement opportunities.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Report Builder" icon="file-text">
    Transform your rough notes or voice recordings into clear reports with decisions and actions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
