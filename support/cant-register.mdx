<Warning>

- **Embuild**  
- **Bouwunie**

## Common Access Issues

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Email Not Recognized" icon="message">
**Message shown:** "Email address not recognized"

**How to fix it:**
1. Confirm you have an active membership with one of the partner organizations  
2. Ask your organization to check whether your **email is correctly listed**  
3. If your email has recently changed, request an update to your member profile  
4. Non-members may request access, but approval requires manual review  
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Login Attempts Blocked" icon="lock">
**Message shown:** "Too many login attempts"

**How to fix it:**
1. Wait 15 minutes before retrying  
2. Clear your browser cache and cookies  
3. Try another browser or an incognito/private window  
4. Contact support if the issue continues  
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Verification Email Issues" icon="clock">
**Problem:** Verification email not received or link expired

**How to fix it:**
1. Check your spam/junk folder  
2. Request a new verification email  
3. Make sure you're using the correct address  
4. Prefer your **official company email domain** rather than a private email  
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
