## What Tooli Does

Think of Tooli as a construction-smart helper you can talk to naturally.  
Ask questions, upload documents, get clear explanations, and refine as needed.

<Steps>
<Step title="Ask Questions">
Ask in Dutch, French, German, or English. Tooli understands construction terms and Belgian regulations.
</Step>

<Step title="Upload Files">
Send your project documents—specs, BoQs, offers, photos, plans. Tooli handles long, complex construction files easily.
</Step>

<Step title="Get Clear Answers">
Receive practical explanations tailored to your work and your language.
</Step>

<Step title="Ask Follow-up Questions">
Refine, clarify, and dig deeper. Tooli follows your context across the conversation.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Your Expert Agents

Each agent acts like a digital colleague with a permanent role and method.  
Switch to the one that fits your task.

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="General Assistant" icon="message">
    Your go-to helper for any construction question—materials, standards, regulations, planning, or document interpretation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Offer Coach" icon="check-circle">
