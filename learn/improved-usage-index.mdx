## Learn How to Get the Best Out of Tooli

The **Learning** section is your toolbox for becoming truly effective with Tooli.  
Whether you're preparing quotes, checking BoQs, structuring Excel files, or guiding a meeting, the way you ask questions and manage conversations makes a huge difference.

These guides teach you how to brief Tooli the same way you’d brief an experienced colleague — with clear instructions, the right context, and simple back-and-forth.

<Info>
Practical, construction-oriented, and made for busy professionals — no technical background needed.
</Info>
