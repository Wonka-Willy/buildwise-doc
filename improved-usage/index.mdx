## Quick Tips for Construction Professionals

<CardGroup cols={3}>

Always include project type, location, budget, and timeline in your prompts.
</Card>

<Card title="Choose the Right Agent" icon="users">
Match your task to the specialist - Quote Builder for pricing, Quality Coach for reviews.

Start fresh conversations for new projects or when responses decline in quality.

## Common Construction Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Project Planning & Quoting">
**Best Agents:** General Assistant, Quote Builder, Quality Coach
**Strategy:** Use General Assistant for initial guidance, Quote Builder for pricing, Quality Coach for review
**Tips:** Include all project constraints upfront, iterate through refinements
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Document Review & Analysis">
**Best Agents:** BoQ Checker, Quality Coach
**Strategy:** Upload documents with specific analysis requests
**Tips:** Focus on one document type per conversation, ask for specific error types
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Project Changes & Variations">
**Best Agents:** Variation Builder, General Assistant
**Strategy:** Provide original scope, change details, and desired formality level
**Tips:** Include timeline and cost impact requirements, specify contract references
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Meeting Documentation">
**Best Agents:** Report Builder
**Strategy:** Upload notes or voice recordings with context about meeting type
**Tips:** Include attendee roles, project phase, and required follow-up format
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
