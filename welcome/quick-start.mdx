## Getting Started

<Steps>
<Step title="Visit Tooli">
Access Tooli through multiple entry points:

- **Direct access:** Go to [tooli.be](https://tooli.be)
- **From Buildwise:** Navigate from buildwise.be to the AI tools section
- **Organization portal:** Use your organization's specific Tooli link
- **Landing page:** Through partner organization websites

<Tip>
Bookmark the site in your favorites for quick access from the construction site or office.
</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Registration & Verification">
**Fill in your details:**
- Full name and display name (how others see you)
- Professional email address
- Password (8+ characters with letters and numbers)

**Automatic verification:**
- Your email is checked against partner organization whitelists
- **If whitelisted:** Instant access approved
- **If not whitelisted:** Submit access request for manual review

<Note>
Premium members of Buildwise, Embuild, Bouwunie, and Constructiv are typically auto-approved.
</Note>
</Step>

<Step title="Organization Setup">
**Based on your email, you'll either:**

- **Join existing organization** - Select from list if your company is already registered
- **Create new organization** - Set up name, slug (your-slug.tooli.be) for new teams

<Tip>
Large companies often have existing organizations you can join immediately.
</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Accept Policies">
On your first login, you'll review and accept:
- **Terms of Service** - Platform usage rules
- **Privacy Policy** - How your data is handled  
- **Data Retention Policy** - 12-month automatic deletion
- **EU Processing Notice** - GDPR compliance information

<Note>
Quick one-time setup to ensure you understand data handling and usage rules.
</Note>
</Step>

<Step title="Welcome Tour">
The guided tour shows you:
- **Interface overview** - Chat area, agent panel, settings
- **Input methods** - Type or speak your questions
- **Mode selection** - Fast vs Thinking explained
- **File upload** - How to add documents

<Tip>
You can skip the tour and revisit it later from settings if needed.
</Tip>
</Step>

<Step title="Your First Conversation">
**Choose an agent and start chatting:**

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="General Assistant" icon="message-square">
**Try:** "What's the minimum concrete strength for foundations?"
</Card>

<Card title="Quote Builder" icon="calculator">
**Try:** "Create a quote for bathroom renovation, 2x3m"
</Card>
</CardGroup>

- Pick any agent in the right panel
- Type your question naturally
- Get instant, construction-specific answers
- Ask follow-up questions as needed

<Note>
Start with Fast Mode (default) for quick answers. Try Thinking Mode for complex analysis once you're comfortable.
</Note>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Common First Questions

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Is my data secure?" icon="shield">
Yes - 100% EU hosting, GDPR compliant, encrypted storage, no data used for AI training.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I delete conversations?" icon="trash">
Yes - Delete individual chats anytime, or they auto-delete after 12 months of inactivity.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if the answer is wrong?" icon="alert">
Always verify critical information. Tooli provides sources and disclaimers. For legal advice, consult your organization or Buildwise.be.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I share conversations?" icon="share">
Yes - Create secure, private share links that aren't indexed by search engines.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How much does it cost?" icon="dollar-sign">
Access is included with your partner organization membership. Usage limits may apply.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
