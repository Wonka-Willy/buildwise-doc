## Choosing the Right Agent

<Steps>
<Step title="Identify Your Task">
**Ask yourself:** What do I need to accomplish?
- Creating something new → **Quote Builder** or **Variation Builder**  
- Checking existing work → **BoQ Checker** or **Quality Coach**
- Organizing information → **Report Builder**
- General questions → **General Assistant**
</Step>

<Step title="Select and Start">
**In the right panel:** Click on the appropriate agent
**Start naturally:** Type your request as you would ask a colleague
</Step>

<Step title="Switch Anytime">
**Need different expertise?** Click another agent
**Context preserved:** They'll understand what you're working on
</Step>
</Steps>

## Pro Tips for Working with Agents

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Be Specific" icon="bullseye">
**Instead of:** *"Help with quote"*
**Try:** *"Small bathroom renovation, 2x3m, tiles and plumbing, modern style"*
</Card>

<Card title="Upload Context" icon="upload">
**Include relevant files** when possible
- Project plans, specifications, photos
- Existing quotes or documents to review
- Meeting notes or voice recordings
</Card>

<Card title="Use Follow-ups" icon="reply">
**Ask for clarification:** *"Can you explain the planning section in more detail?"*
**Request variations:** *"Make this more formal for client presentation"*
</Card>

<Card title="Combine Agents" icon="users">
**Example workflow:**
1. **General Assistant** for initial advice
2. **Quote Builder** to create the offer  
3. **Quality Coach** to review before sending
</Card>
</CardGroup>
